EASY RECIPES

Omelette

  • 3 eggs
  • Oil for the pan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Whisk the eggs. Season with salt and pepper.
Fry with oil on medium heat until it's cooked
all the way through. Enjoy!

Pancakes

  • 1 egg
  • 1 dl of flour
  • 2 dl milk
  • Butter
  • Salt

Whisk together the egg and milk.
Add the flour and whisk it together.
Fry in butter on medium heat -
1 dl of batter at a time.
Serve with jelly or sugar and cinnamon.

Greek Salad

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Black olives
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Chop all the vegetables to preferred size.
Mix the oil with the salt and pepper.
Add the vegetables to a bowl and drizzle
the oil over it.
