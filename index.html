Omelette

  • 3 eggs
  • Oil for the pan
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Whisk the eggs. Season with salt and pepper. Fry with oil on medium heat until it's cooked all the way through. Enjoy!

Pancakes

  • 1 egg
  • 1 dl of flour
  • 2 dl milk
  • Butter
  • Salt

Whisk together the egg and milk. Add the flour and whisk it together. Fry in butter on medium heat - 1 dl of batter at a time. Serve with jelly or sugar and cinnamon.

Greek Salad

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Black olives
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Chop all the vegetables to preferred size. Mix the oil with the salt and pepper. Add the vegetables to a bowl and drizzle the oil over it.

Mac & cheese

  • 1 dl of macaroni
  • 25 g of butter
  • 1,5 tsp of flour
  • 8 dl milk
  • 200 g cheese

Cook the macaroni as instructed on the box. Melt the butter and whisk together with the flour. Slowly add the milk and whisk. Add the cheese and let it melt. Mix in the macaroni.
